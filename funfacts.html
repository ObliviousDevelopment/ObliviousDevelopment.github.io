Trivia

□ When Saban Entermainment recieved the liscencing for the english version of the series, transcripts/information regarding the contents of the episodes were either missing or poorly translated.
To remedy this producer Andy Thomas and his production team watched audioless episodes in no particular order and wrote a story based off of what was being seen

□ The Villainous character Bad Max's name is a play on the movie character Mad Max

□ In-show, Seymour Cheese (also known as The Big Cheese) is known to be a horrible singer.
Ironically his English voice actor Dean Hagopian started out as the lead singer for the Canadian rock band *The Stacattos*

□ Much like how the English names for all the characters on references to pop culture,
in the Spanish version the cast is named after pre-existing characters from different media

□ The main characters were almost in a fighting game for the Wii called Tatsunoko vs. Capcom: Ultimate All-Stars.
The game's producer said that many people asked for Samurai Pizza Cats characters. But they couldn't agree to include them.

□ The Rude Noise (A team of villains in the series) are counterparts to the Rescue Team, a cast of side characters that make appearances every now and then that aid the main trio
